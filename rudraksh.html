Mukhi Rudraksha

Mukhi Rudraksha is also known as the king of all Rudrakshas. As per Shiva Puran, it is considered the most auspicious of all Rudrakshas. Lord Shiva says that one who wears 1 Mukhi Rudraksha is spiritually elevated and gets all the materialistic comforts of life. The shape of the original 1 Mukhi rudraksha bead is cashew nut or half-moon, and the color is brown. This one Mukhi rudraksha shows the relation between heaven and earth.
